# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- being respectful and constructive
- giving and receiving feedback gracefully
- focusing on the best outcome for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- personal attacks or trolling
- sharing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

Report concerns by opening a private security report or contacting maintainers directly.
